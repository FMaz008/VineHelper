Pages Auto-Load
Interval
Automatically load pages when the notifications monitor is active. This allow you to define the intervals between page load. The interval will be picked at random between the selected range. When triggered, VH will open and close a Vine page in a separate tab automatically.

Active time range
Limit to auto-load behavior to a time range. Has to be at least 8 hours of range otherwise the range will default to 3AM to 5PM.
